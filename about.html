About this site

This is a project to bring together different tools and resources for makers and makerspaces in libraries that can be edited, re-used and remixed as required.

It’s collaborative and open and we’re always looking for new contributions. These recipes are designed to help you get started, they’re by no means meant to be followed to the letter.

Content has been contributed by Artefacto, Cybercitizens, Makercart and more.

The site was built by Kate

You can contribute via GitHub.
