Get involved


Contributions welcome

We are always looking for contributions of any kind.

If you've run a workshop or worked on a maker project that you'd like to share, we'd love to hear from you.

You can contribute by email, or via Github.

To share your content or if you'd like to discuss it with us, get in touch.

Also, if you have any questions, corrections or suggestions about the content on LibraryMakers.net, please open an issue on GitHub.
